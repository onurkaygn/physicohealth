Why Physico ?

Physico is an innovative health application developed by three students. This application aims to make healthcare services more accessible and manageable for everyone, not just for doctors and patients.

Thanks to Physico, patients no longer need to be physically present with their doctors for face-to-face consultations. This feature is especially convenient for patients with limited mobility or those living far away. Patients can communicate securely with their doctors through in-app messaging without sharing their phone numbers or personal information. This offers significant advantages in terms of both privacy and ease of use. Additionally, Physico provides personalized health and treatment programs to users, eliminating the need for other applications. Users can follow their programs correctly by watching exercise and movement videos within the app.
